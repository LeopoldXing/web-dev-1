11 Communication Strategies

Communication strategies will make your interactions more intentional and will help you communicate your Human Skills in class, at work, and at home. Click on each icon below to learn how.

Advocating

Develop the confidence to actively support a person or perspective.

Clarifying

Repeat or rephrase information to ensure that the intended message has been received.

Investigating

Making Decisions

Determine appropriate responses to a situation and apply them to ensure goals are met.

Organizing

Create and use processes to organize and sort information.

Participating

Engage with others to ensure communication is clear, correct, and complete.

Planning

Use a process to plan out communications to ensure they are complete, correct, and clear.

Questioning

Ask questions throughout a process to ensure needs are met.

Reflecting

Pre- and post-reflect to assess, repair, and improve outcomes.

Repairing

Repair and correct miscommunication and difficult situations.

Setting Goals

Establish individual and collaborative goals and responsibilities.
